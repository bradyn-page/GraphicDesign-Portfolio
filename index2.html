About Me

I am a Graphic Designer with a degree in Communication Technology from the University of Texas at Arlington. I specialize in branding, digital design, and social media visuals, as well as UI/UX design. I enjoy creating clean, modern & retro visuals that help brands communicate clearly and effectively.

My focus is on building strong visual storytelling through typography, layout, and color systems and focusing on people-centered design.

Graphic Designs

Anna Pavlova Poster

Designed a vintage-inspired promotional poster celebrating the legacy of ballerina Anna Pavlova. The composition combines bold typography, distressed textures, and layered color separations to create a retro screen-printed aesthetic. Motion blur and repeated silhouettes convey movement and energy, while the striking yellow, red, and black palette evokes the look of early 20th-century performance posters and contemporary grunge design.

Bacardi Concept Redesign

Created a redesign of the classic Bacardi company logo, consisting of a decorative monogram and custom wordmark accented with celestial symbols and illustrative elements. The identity draws inspiration from vintage typography and cosmic themes to convey a brand personality that is elegant, imaginative, and uniquely memorable.

Retro Coffee Logo Design

Designed a visual identity for a retro coffee shop inspired by 1970s Americana and classic diner aesthetics. Featuring bold typography and nostalgic design elements, the branding captures the warmth and familiarity of gathering over a cup of coffee while maintaining a modern, versatile feel for print and digital applications.

Shadow Typography Design

A typographic study examining how light and shadow influence form, readability, and visual rhythm. By experimenting with depth and contrast, the project explores the ways shadows can create dimension and mood while enhancing the interaction between negative space and letterforms.
